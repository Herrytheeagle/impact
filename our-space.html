Located at the Ikoyi -14 Gerard Road, Impact Hub Lagos is the perfect place for any event from a high-profile launch to an intimate creative workshop. Impact Hub Lagos offers 1500 square miles dedicated event space with with several private offices and events spaces. The event spaces can hold Over 200 people and all rooms are equipped with suitable amenities. Check out the room details below to find out what space fits best for your next event at Impact Hub Lagos

Private Offices

We have numerous private offices. They differ in size accommodating a range of 2 - 12 people. The total number of seats that can be accommodated is 35.

Outdoor Space

An outdoor event space around the building which can accommodate over 200 people.

Conference Room

A conference room which can take up to 12 people.

Co-working Space

Co-working spaces where people can carry out tasks. Events can also be hosted here. It can accommodate over 60 people.

Training Room

Here people can polish their skills and develop their businesses.

Pergola

This is another outdoor space where events can be held. It is located at the back of the Impact Hub building.
